Beef Sausage     
  • 4 oz. ground beef
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1/2 tsp. minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp. ground fennel
  • 1/2 tsp. pepper
  • 1/2 tsp. pink salt
  • pinch of red pepper flakes
  • pinch of cayenne pepper

Mix spices together in a large bowl.
Add meat and mix thoroughly.
Form into patty.
Brown meat on stovetop until cooked through.
